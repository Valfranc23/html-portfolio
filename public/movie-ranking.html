Valencia's Go-To Movies

Here's a look at my top 3 favorite films

Interview with the Vampire

I love this movie in part because Brad Pitt & Tom Cruise played their parts well. The overall film adaptation of the book was also done well

Harry Potter Film 5: Order of the Phoenix

As another book-to-film adaptation, this one was one of the closest to the original book.

Alice in Wonderland

The world building in Disney's live action remake of this film is a lot of fun to look at.
